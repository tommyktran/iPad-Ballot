Voting Instructions

There are 3 major steps in going through the HTML ballot:

  1. Making ballot selections
  2. Reviewing ballot selections
  3. Reading and signing the accessible absentee ballot affirmation

These 3 major steps are under Heading 1, while the ballot races are under Heading 2.

You can use the up and down arrow keys to browse through the ballot. Use key 1 and 2 in combination with the Shift key to quickly get to the next or previous heading.

Please note that the first two races on this ballot are ranked-choice races, each of which is presented in a table with the candidate names in the left most column.

While making ballot selections, if you get to a checkbox for the option you wish to choose, such as the name of a candidate or the Yes or No response to a question, use the space bar to check that checkbox.

While reviewing ballot selections, you will hear your selections for one race at a time, hit the space bar if your want to change your vote for that race.
